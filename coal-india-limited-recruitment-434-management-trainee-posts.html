OAL India Limited is inviting applications for 434 Management Trainee (MT) posts, providing a great opportunity for young candidates aspiring to work in a prestigious organization. Here's a detailed overview of the posts and their eligibility criteria:

Who Can Apply?

Community Development Management Trainee:

  • Postgraduate Degree or Diploma in Community Development, Rural Development, Community Organization & Development Practice, Urban & Rural Community Development, or related fields.
  • At least 60% marks from a recognized institution.

Environmental Management Trainee:

  • First-Class Degree in Environmental Engineering or a related qualification.

For other posts, candidates can refer to the official notification for detailed eligibility criteria.

Age Limit

  • The maximum age limit is 30 years.
  • Reserved category candidates will receive age relaxation as per government rules.

Selection Process

  • Selection will be based on performance in the Computer-Based Test (CBT).

Salary

  • Selected candidates will receive a monthly salary of ₹60,000 – ₹1,80,000.

How to Apply?

  • Applications must be submitted online.
  • Last date to apply: February 14, 2025.
